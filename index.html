Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  App: Nutri AI Tracker

In short: Nutri AI Tracker stores all your personal data (profile, meals, goals) exclusively on your device. There is no proprietary server and no user account. Certain app features send anonymised requests to third-party services (Groq AI, Open Food Facts, Google AdMob) — full details below.

2. What Data Is Processed?

The app processes two categories of data: data that remains exclusively on your device, and data that is transmitted to external services for specific features.

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored using AsyncStorage (local device storage) and does not leave your device automatically:

  • User profile: sex, age, height (cm), weight (kg), activity level
  • Calorie goal & macro targets (protein, carbohydrates, fat)
  • Nutrition diary: logged meals with date, time, food items, calorie values and nutritional data
  • Custom product database: food items you create manually, including nutritional values and optional barcode
  • Groq API key: entered manually by you; stored only locally and used for direct requests from your device to Groq
  • App settings: language, enabled features (e.g. AI forecast), calorie history

This data is accessible only to you. It is not transmitted to the app developer or any other server.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Local storage (profile, nutrition diary): Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – processing necessary for the performance of the service contract (provision of app features).
  • Groq API (AI features): Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR – consent; AI features are optional and require you to enter your own API key. Note: photos and audio data may contain biometric or health-related information; you decide independently which images you send to Groq.
  • Open Food Facts: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – necessary to provide the barcode search feature; the transmitted data is not personal data.
  • Google AdMob: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR – consent. Where required by law, the app will request your consent before showing personalised ads.

5. International Data Transfers

Groq Inc. and Google LLC are based in the United States. Transfers are carried out on the basis of Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46(2)(c) GDPR) and, where applicable, the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework. For further details, please refer to the privacy policies of the respective providers linked above.

6. Health Data

Body-related data such as weight, height, age and calorie goals constitute special categories of personal data under Art. 9 GDPR. This data is processed exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to the app developer.

Photos you send to the Groq API for AI analysis may indirectly contain health-related information (e.g. meals that indicate dietary habits). Processing only occurs when you actively choose to use the photo analysis feature.

7. Data Deletion

All locally stored data can be deleted completely by uninstalling the app. Within the app, you can delete individual entries at any time.

For data processed by third parties (Groq, Google), their own retention periods apply. Please contact the respective providers directly to request deletion of data stored with them.
